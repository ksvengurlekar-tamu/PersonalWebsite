Why Computer Science?

I chose the Computer Science career path because both of my parents, and even my grandfather, pursued careers in tech. I grew up learning to think logically and technically like they did. When I first began taking courses in math and programming, it came naturally to me. Now that I am older, I can apply the human aspect to the work I've been doing in school. I am starting to understand the impact and the reason behind the demand for new developments. I am so facinated by how a few seconds of algorithmic run time are worth millions of dollars from companies trying to optimize their products. I want to take after my parents and grandpa and be a part of the process that changes the world.
