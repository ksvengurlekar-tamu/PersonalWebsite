Experience and Skills

My skills include programming in languages like Java, C++, Python, and JavaScript. These are used in coursework areas such as data structures, algorithms, operating system design, and computer architecture. Through coursework, I studied the hardware components of processors and internal machine-level processes when running programs. I am proficient in X-86 assembly instructions because it was required to learn how compilation occurs internally with the whole system architecture. Through my operating system course, I studied Linux/Unix commands and functionality when interacting with running processes. This includes concurrency, parallelism, and data pipes. I am also proficient in front-end development software such as Angular.JS and Node.JS. I am experienced with using HMTL and CSS formatting to build web pages. I am pursuing a minor concurrent to my bachelor's degree in math. My coursework for this minor includes Calculus I, II, and III, Linear Algebra, Discrete Math, and Differential Equations.

Why Computer Science?

I chose the Computer Science career path because both of my parents and even my grandfather pursued careers in tech. I grew up learning to think logically and technically like they did. When I first began taking courses in math and programming, it came naturally to me. The assignments I was given felt like puzzles, and I was able to think through them fluidly. Now that I am older, I can apply the human aspect to the work I've been doing in school. I am starting to understand the impact and the reason behind the demand for new developments. I am so fascinated by how a few seconds of algorithmic run time are worth millions of dollars to companies trying to optimize their products. I can clearly visualize how the newest technological advancements impact everyone's day-to-day. I want to follow in the footsteps of my parents and grandpa and be a part of the process that changes the world.

I can tell that this industry has the most potential in it. I personally want to be a pioneer in this industry with something creative, innovative, and essential for society in the future.
